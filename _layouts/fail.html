About FAIL!

FAIL! is an initiative started from an international group of students at Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT). FAIL! has inspired people in Europe, and North America and now we are proud to bring it to Asia.

FAIL! is an event aimed at embracing failures rather than giving up at them, hiding them, or ignoring the lessons they teach us. It is a celebration of the moments we generally don't share.

Failure can happen to anyone, anywhere, and is a key to success. A misstep can happen to even professionals, experts, CEOâ€™s, or professors. We bring them to FAIL! to share the meaning of their personal and professional failures and to understand the deeper meaning of failures.

We have invited Indian youth icons from all over the country as far as Jammu to talk about the times they've had a setback in their lives, when they've fallen face first, and how they recovered and learned from these experiences.

Ajay Kumar Siotra

Ajay Kumar Siotra, one of Jammu's budding Entrepreneurs and owner of Cafe Scoorervala. An inspiration to many!

His passion for cooking reflected in his food. His MasterChef India apron was a mark of his determination.

Hurdling through the societal norms, Siotra followed his dreams. Elimination from the competition was a fall, but that definitely was his stepping stone to a bright future.

He will be sharing his experiences and failures on 2nd March 2019 at IIIT, Hyderabad.

Pankhuri Gidwani

Pankhuri Gidwani is the face of the new generation. As the 2nd runner up in Miss India 2016, and the brand ambassador of Women Power Line 1090, she's a testament to the fact that age is no barrier for making a mark.

Listen to her at FAIL! where she will be sharing her experiences and failures on 2nd March 2019 at IIIT, Hyderabad.
